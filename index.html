About Us

We are Troop 76 of Ballardvale in Andover. During our regular meetings, we do all kinds of activities. Some of these include rank advancement, trip planning and learning scout skills. We go on all sorts of trips together, from rockclimbing to biking to camping. There is always a fun event happening soon. In the summer, we attend Camp Wanocksett in Dublin, NH.

When do we meet?

We meet Fridays from 7-8:30 PM in the lower level at Ballardvale United Church. Visitors are welcome to join us for our meetings at any time.
